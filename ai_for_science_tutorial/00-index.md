# AI for Science: Accelerating Scientific Discovery

## Overview
Learn how AI is transforming scientific discovery. From AlphaFold's protein folding to automated experiment design, AI is becoming essential for scientific research.

2. **[Drug Discovery](02-drug-discovery.md)** - Molecular property prediction, generative models for molecules, virtual screening, clinical trial optimization
3. **[Materials Design](03-materials-design.md)** - Crystal structure prediction, materials informatics, inverse design, high-throughput screening
4. **[Physics-Informed ML](04-physics-informed-ml.md)** - PINNs, operator learning, solving PDEs, conservation laws, hybrid models
5. **[Scientific Reasoning](05-scientific-reasoning.md)** - Hypothesis generation, literature mining, automated theorem proving, scientific discovery
6. **[Automated Experiment Design](06-experiment-design.md)** - Bayesian optimization, active learning, closed-loop experimentation, lab automation
