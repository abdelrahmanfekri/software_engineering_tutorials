# PhD-Level Tutorial: Shariah Compliance & Business Knowledge for Islamic Product-Based Finance AI

## Overview

This tutorial provides doctoral-level depth on Shariah compliance and business knowledge for building AI-driven product-based financing systems in Islamic finance. It bridges classical fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence), contemporary governance standards (AAOIFI, IFSB), and the commercial realities of Islamic banks and fintechs.

**Target audience**: Founders, engineers, and product leads building Islamic finance AI; researchers in Islamic fintech.

**Prerequisites**: Basic familiarity with Islamic finance structures (Murabaha, Musharakah, etc.); interest in product-based/asset-backed financing.

## Key Standards Referenced

- **AAOIFI** — Accounting and Auditing Organization for Islamic Financial Institutions (Shariah Standards, FAS)
- **IFSB** — Islamic Financial Services Board (Guiding Principles, Capital Adequacy)
- **Shariah Governance Standard on Generative AI** (2025)
- **Islamic Fiqh Academy** — OIC Fiqh Academy rulings
- **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM)** — Shariah Advisory Council resolutions
- **S&P / MSCI / FTSE** — Shariah screening methodologies

## Learning Path

1. **Foundations first** (01–02): Understand prohibitions and structures before governance.
2. **Governance** (03, 05): How institutions operationalize compliance and how AI must align.
3. **Product focus** (04): Map product/asset risk to Shariah structures.
4. **Business & regulation** (06–07): Market reality and regulatory constraints.
